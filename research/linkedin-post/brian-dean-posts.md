## Post 1
[Most people are missing how AI is ACTUALLY changing SEO.

Back in the day, SEO was all about things like:

-On-page optimization

-Keyword targeting

-Trying to rank #1 for a specific keyword

Obviously, these things still exist. 

(If you rank #1 In Google for “credit cards” you can pretty much buy a private island... LOL)

And that’s true whether LLMs are around or not.

But what HAS changed is that these old school SEO approaches aren’t enough. 

Because they don’t always help you rank in AI answers. And that's where the huge upside is. 

Why?

Well, look at how search behavior and the SERPs themselves have changed: 

-AI answers appear above the traditional SERPs

-Zero-click search is now the default

-Traffic doesn’t always correlate with ranking position

In short:

SEO is almost like social media now… where it’s all about getting eyeballs to your site (with the occasional high-intent click).
]
