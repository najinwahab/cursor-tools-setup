# Importance of Topical Authority for SEO

**Author**: Koray Tuğberk Gübür

**Document Type**: Original Research / Foundational Framework Article

**Source**: Holistic SEO Digital

**URL**: https://www.holisticseo.digital/theoretical-seo/topical-authority/

**Date**: Feb 11, 2021 

## Summary
This is Koray's foundational article where he coined the term **Topical
Authority** — a semantic SEO methodology built around processing connected
topics and entailed search queries with accurate, unique, and expert
information. The article documents real case studies showing growth from 0 to
128,000 organic visits in 123 days, achieved without backlinks or traditional
technical SEO.

The piece is essential reading because it proves that content depth, entity
relationships, and topical map structure — not keyword density or link
building — are what drive sustainable rankings. This thinking now directly
shapes how LLMs index and cite content, making it one of the most prescient
frameworks in modern AI-powered SEO.
