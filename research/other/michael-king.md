# How AI Mode Works and How SEO Can Prepare for the Future of Search

**Author**: Mike King

**Document Type**: Technical Deep-Dive / Original Research Article

**Source**: iPullRank

**URL**: https://ipullrank.com/how-ai-mode-works

**Date**: May 27, 2025

## Summary

Mike King delivers the most technically rigorous public breakdown of Google's
AI Mode published in 2025, drawing directly from four Google patent
applications to explain how AI Mode actually works under the hood. He
documents the full pipeline: query fan-out (where a single search expands into
dozens of hidden synthetic queries), passage-level dense retrieval using vector
embeddings, multi-stage LLM orchestration, and user embedding-based
personalization — where every result is shaped by a vector representation of
the individual user's behavioral history across Google's entire ecosystem.

The core argument is urgent and direct: "This is not just SEO. It's what comes
after SEO." King shows that legacy SEO tools (built on TF-IDF and BM25 sparse
retrieval) are fundamentally misaligned with how AI Mode selects and cites
content. To compete, brands must engineer content at the passage level for
semantic similarity, anticipate synthetic query landscapes, and optimize for
LLM pairwise passage comparison — where your content is judged head-to-head
against competitors by a reasoning model, not a deterministic scoring function.

# How to Show Up in AI Results with Relevance Engineering

**Author**:  Vince Nero

**Document Type**: Written Interview / Article

**Source**: BuzzStream Blog

**URL**: https://www.buzzstream.com/blog/relevance-engineering-podcast/

**Date**: September 26, 2025

## Summary

Mike King explains his concept of Relevance Engineering — the intersection
of content strategy, information retrieval science, and AI optimization. He
covers how his proprietary tool Qforia reveals hidden AI queries that
traditional keyword tools cannot detect, helping brands identify and target
the exact passages LLMs extract during generative search.

Key practical takeaways: Digital PR must shift from generic outreach to
mathematically defined topical relevance, short factual paragraphs dramatically
improve LLM content extraction, and brands need to think in passages rather
than pages when optimizing for AI recommendation.
