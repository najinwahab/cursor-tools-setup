# Moving Beyond Old SEO Models in the Age of AI | Kevin Indig

**Author**: Gianluca Fiorelli

**Document Type**: Long-Form Interview / Written Article

**Source**: Advanced Web Ranking

**URL**: https://www.advancedwebranking.com/blog/beyond-old-seo-models-ai-search

**Date**: January 19, 2025

## Summary

Kevin Indig breaks down why keyword-based mental models are becoming obsolete
as AI search expands. He covers entity-driven SEO, brand content as AI
training strategy, and how surfacing internal documents boosts LLM
discoverability and defends brand reputation through context engineering.

Key shifts highlighted: SEOs are evolving from strategic advisors to hands-on
operators who build content pipelines and internal tools directly. The role is
moving from optimization to construction — a theme central to AI-powered SEO
content production.

# 7 SEO, Marketing, and Tech Predictions for 2026

**Author**: Kevin Indig

**Document Type**: Predictions / Opinion Piece

**Source**: Search Engine Journal

**URL**: https://www.searchenginejournal.com/seo-marketing-and-tech-predictions-for-2026/562373/

**Date**: December 3, 2025

## Summary
Kevin Indig's 2026 predictions cover the end of AI dashboards, the rise of
agentic SEO workflows, and the emerging split between a bot-facing web and a
verified human-facing web. He argues winning teams will merge Brand, PR, and
Technical SEO into unified squads focused on outcomes.

The piece is particularly relevant for understanding how AI content production
pipelines will be evaluated not by volume but by systemic influence —
reinforcing his Growth Memo thesis that metrics are broken and systems
thinking is the new competitive advantage.
